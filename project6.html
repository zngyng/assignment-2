    • "The wheather is changing
    PLEASE,
    TURN IT OFF."

    This is a project about social problem. The problem I pick is "Cliamte Change".
    The earth is being hurt and I am making different sizes of bandages to "heal the earth".
    On the bandages, there are informations that ask people to do something they can to slow down the speed of climate change.
    The information inside the bandage is "-9F° Summer" and "99F° Winter" can actually happen if we don't do something.
    The way I test this idea is to begin with turning off the lights when people leave.
    I make touchable signs beside the switches.
    Also, I make little bandages which can actually stick and hand them out and ask people to stick wherever they think need improve.
    This was to draw the audience's attention to take part in the climate bandage project.
